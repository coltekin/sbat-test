Overview

Please enter your label set, one label per line. For hierarchical labels, enter subordinate labels underneath their parent label and begin the line of the subordinate label with a >

For example:
Person
>Politician

For multi-class classification, write the classname on a line beginning with a *, and write the labels underneath, beginning each line with a -.

For example:
*Sentiment
-Positive
-Negative
